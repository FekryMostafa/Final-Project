Jeff Schatten, assistant professor of business administration at Washington and Lee University, recently interviewed W&L President Will Dudley in his podcast, “Demystifying Organizations.”
In general, the podcast features conversations with the world’s leading management thinkers and executives on a broad range of topics that are vital to organizations.
In the podcast, Schatten and Dudley discuss organizational culture and values.
