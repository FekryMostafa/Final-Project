FALL CHORAL CONCERTFEATURING THE UNIVERSITY SINGERS, THE GLEE CLUB AND CANTATRICI
Tuesday, October 24, 2023 at 8 p.m.Wilson Concert Hall/Lenfest Center
Tickets are free, but required. Tickets become available Tuesday, September 12.• Performance will be streamed.https://livestream.com/wlu
The first half of the concert will be centered around Latvian composer Laura Jekabsone's Sudraba Mēness and Mendelssohn's I Waited for the Lord, which will feature music majors Sarah Gaberielle Lynch '24 and Meredith Harron '26 on the solos. The second half will feature the award-winning University Singers, conducted by Shane M. Lynch, as they present a set on Light, Stars and Fire, featuring the stunning work Stomp on the Fire by Andrea Ramsey, followed by a set of accompanied music that closes with Rossini's fabulous I Gondolieri featuring Dr. Anna Billias on the piano. Traditional favorites will round out the evening, finally closing with James Erb's Shenandoah.
