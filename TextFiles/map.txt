Lexington, VA is made up of vibrant, historic and charming communities located in the western foothills of Virginia’s Blue Ridge Mountains. As the the southern gateway to the Shenandoah Valley we are the perfect destination for a “small towns, big backyard” road trip. Here, visitors will find endless opportunity for outdoor adventure, historic sites, unique arts and cultural experiences, entertainment, and complete relaxation. Getting here is easy! Lexington, Buena Vista and Rockbridge County are conveniently located in the Shenandoah Valley, easily accessible and all within a day’s drive of every major east coast city via I-81 or I-64.
