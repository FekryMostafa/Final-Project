By Erica TurmanSeptember 12, 2019
Lucas Morel, professor of politics and head of the Politics Department at Washington and Lee University, was interviewed about Ralph Ellison’s novel “Invisible Man” by John J. Miller, a national correspondent for “National Review” and the director of the Dow Journalism Program at Hillsdale College.
The episode aired on Miller’s “The Great Books” podcast series.
