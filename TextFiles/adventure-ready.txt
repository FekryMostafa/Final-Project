Scramble across Devil’s Marbleyard. Stagger up House Mountain. Or mingle with thru-hikers on the white-blazed Appalachian Trail as it unfurls along the Blue Ridge Parkway.
Exploring by bicycle, motorcycle and horseback is an immersive adventure in Rockbridge County.
Home to creeks, rivers, lakes, and reservoirs, plus a variety of cold and warm water fish, Rockbridge County wins Best All Around for fishing in Virginia.
On September 24, 2016, Natural Bridge, a National Historic Landmark, became Natural Bridge State Park​ to become the 37th jewel in the crown of the Virginia State Parks collection.
