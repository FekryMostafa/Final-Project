By W&L News OfficeDecember 17, 2021
Jamie Goodin ’10, digital engagement manager at Washington and Lee University, was awarded the Junior Chamber of Commerce (Jaycees) Member of the Year Award at the Lexington-Rockbridge chapter’s annual holiday party in December 2021.
A national organization, the Jaycees was founded in the late 1920s to provide development opportunities that empower young people to create positive change. Goodin is also the 2022 president of the Lexington-Rockbridge Jaycees.
