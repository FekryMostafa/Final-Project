The University makes no provisions for delivery of U.S. Postal Service mail to students. In order for on-campus residents to receive mail from the U.S. Postal Service, students must rent a box at the local Post Office. All packages from other carriers (UPS, FedEx, Airborne, etc.) will be delivered to the W&L Mail Services office.
All Washington and Lee students, faculty and staff members, subject to certain restrictions, are permitted to own and operate motor vehicles at the University during the academic year. Due to construction projects anticipated during the academic year, some parking areas may not be fully available. Full cooperation of all drivers will be important, and we ask that you not bring motor vehicles to the campus unless absolutely necessary.
Traveller offers students a safe ride within five miles of campus, seven nights a week from 10 p.m. to 2 a.m., using a combination of scheduled bus routes and dispatch services.
Students are permitted to ride the Maury Express bus, which serves Lexington, Buena Vista and Rockbridge County, free with their valid University ID. The Maury Express provides transportation to local shopping destinations including Kroger and Walmart. Transportation to nearby airports, train and bus stations in Roanoke, Charlottesville and Staunton can be arranged through the University's campus classified ads as well as outside transportation services.
