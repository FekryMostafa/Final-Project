Each year, the Annual Giving office awards trophies to recognize collective class giving. These trophies recognize outstanding contributions from non-reunion-year classes. Class rankings as of June 30 determine the winners. The prestigious trophies reside in the Hotchkiss House on campus.
Winners are recognized for classes in the following categories:
Leading Class Participation in The President's SocietyAwarded to the non-reunionist undergraduate classes with the highest percentage of members in The President's Society.
Greatest Class Gift Awarded to the non-reunionist undergraduate classes that raise the largest amount of money.
Exceptional Annual Fund Class Participation Awarded to the non-reunionist undergraduate classes with the highest percentage of members participating in the Annual Fund.
Outstanding Class Participation in The 1749 CircleAwarded to the non-reunionist undergraduate classes with the highest percentage of members in the 1749 Circle.
