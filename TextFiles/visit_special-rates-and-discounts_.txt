Monticello's Annual Pass is your ticket to explore Jefferson’s mountaintop year-round.
Area residents can enjoy Monticello's Highlights Tour or Gardens and Grounds Pass all year round with The Local Discount.
Guests who receive benefits through the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP) program can reserve free tour tickets through this program.
Monticello currently extends this offer to undergraduate and graduate students from these two local educational institutions.
Year-round free admission for active-duty military personnel and discounts for veterans are available for select tours and tickets. 
Monticello is proud to offer U.S. military veterans complimentary admission for select tours and passes for Veteran's Day.
