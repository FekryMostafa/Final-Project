Karen Woody has been a professor of law at W&L since July 2019. She has taught Torts, Business Associations, Business Immersion, Securities Regulation, and Insider Trading.  Prior to joining W&L, Professor Woody taught at the Kelley School of Business at Indiana University. She currently lives in Charlottesville, VA.
